Final Project :D

This program is designed to help you decide what type of person you are by selecting options that you like. For example, whether you are a summer person or a winter person by the drinks you like or the clothes you enjoy wearing. You are able to select a questionnaire that you will be able to answer questions and get your results!

Directions:
1. Press the Start Button
2. Select a questionnaire that you would like to browse
3. Select your preference from provided answers
4. Press the Check Button
